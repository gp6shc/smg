With the launch of our new website and brand image, along with our new tagline Simply a Better Way, this is an exciting time at People's Trust!

As part of our brand transformation process, we have identified a clear mission for the future:

To be Florida’s best-prepared home insurance company, delivering the greatest value and a simplified recovery experience to our policyholders.



We are already among the 10 largest home insurance companies in the state of Florida, but we need your help to continue to make People’s Trust the BEST.

Simply a Better Way is more than just a new tagline. It defines who we are and how we aspire to do business every day.

Together, we can be the BEST by living our core values:

Trustworthy

Our policyholders can count on us to deliver on our promises and get them back to pre-loss condition seamlessly. We deal with our policyholders fairly and make sure they understand the People’s Trust approach when they become a policyholder.

Responsive

This means clearly communicating with policyholders and providing them information and service when and where they need it while simplifying and speeding up the recovery process so they can get their life back to normal.

Prepared

We have the physical and fiscal resources, through our affiliation with Rapid Response Team, strong financial reserves and solid reinsurance program, to make sure that we are here for our policyholders when disaster strikes.

Caring

We are there when our policyholders need us most and we treat our customers with warmth, empathy and genuine concern for their well-being; the same way we would want to be treated.

Simplified

We simplify the entire customer experience from getting a quote, choosing coverage, understanding our unique approach, and in the event of a claim making recovery a seamless, no-hassle experience.

We’re only just getting started! Be on the look-out for more information about our brand transformation and what it means to you and our policyholders.
