Now Accepting Fall 2013 Internship Applications

July 11th, 2013 | Written by: Sachs Media Group | share: facebook twitter

Sachs Media Group has a variety of internship positions available for the Fall 2013 semester in Tallahassee and Orlando.

 

Public Relations Intern Description: Are you looking for a fast-paced internship in one of Florida’s dominant public relations firms? Sachs Media Group is seeking self-motivated students to work in both our Tallahassee and Orlando offices. Our interns get the opportunity to work side-by-side with our experienced team of award-winning communicators in an exciting and challenging environment. From writing news releases and organizing news conferences to scripting television spots and pitching stories to local and national media organizations, our interns get unparalleled real-life experience.

Minimum Requirements • Ability to work a minimum of 20 hours per week • Strong writing ability • Knowledge of AP Style • Experience maintaining social media platforms • Familiarity with the Microsoft Office

To apply, please submit your resume and writing sample(s) to Erica Villanueva at internships@sachsmedia.com for opportunities in Tallahassee or Beth Watson bwatson@sachsmedia.com for opportunities in Orlando.

 —

Content Contributor Intern Description: If you love politics, we’ve got an exciting internship opportunity for you. Sachs Media Group is searching for dynamic and energetic interns with exceptional research and writing skills for the fall semester. You can work alongside top-notch, award-winning journalists, track every aspect of Florida’s political landscape and learn politics and communications from the inside out. We’re looking for the best to work on an intensive project alongside our experienced staff.

Minimum Requirements • Ability to work a minimum of 15 hours per week • Strong research and writing ability • Basic knowledge of WordPress •Experience maintaining social media platforms • Familiarity with the Microsoft Office • Graphic design experience not necessary, but strongly desired

To apply, send resume, writing sample and hours available to Trimmel Gomes at trimmel@sachsmedia.com

—

Web Development Intern Description: If you are interested in learning how to create interactive and award-winning online content, Sachs Media Group is searching for dynamic and energetic interns with knowledge of web development. You will have the opportunity to work alongside top-notch, award-winning web developers, who will teach you how to create and manage desktop and mobile friendly websites. Your daily responsibilities will include creating and troubleshooting websites that receive thousands of visitors on a weekly basis, participating in brainstorming sessions with new and current clients, ongoing site maintenance, front-end development of new projects and compiling repositories of workable code.

Minimum Requirements • Ability to work a minimum of 10 hours per week • Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, or PHP • Familiarity with CMS such as WordPress, Drupal or Joomla • Current with latest trends in open source tools and frameworks

To apply, send resume and/or online portfolio to Brian O’Toole at briano@sachsmedia.com

—

Social Media Intern Description: Ready to learn advanced social/digital media strategies and tactics from a team of award-winning online media specialists? Sachs Media Group is looking to add dynamic and hard-working interns with exceptional writing skills and basic knowledge of social media and digital marketing for the fall semester. You will learn how to develop and implement award-winning campaigns for clients in the disciplines of issues management, advocacy, public affairs, B2B and B2C. Your daily activities will include creating sharable online content, monitoring social media communities, analyzing data, researching and developing social/digital advertising campaigns.

Minimum Requirements • Ability to work a minimum of 15 hours per week • Strong research, writing and editing skills • Basic knowledge of Photoshop, graphic design and Microsoft Office • Current with latest trends in digital marketing • Knowledge of web design, UI/UX and public relations preferred

To apply, send link to LinkedIn profile and/or website, as well as work samples, to Ryan Cohn at rcohn@sachsmedia.com
