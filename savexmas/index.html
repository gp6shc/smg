Florida Saves Christmas

Big news this merry season: Thanks to the efforts of Florida's government and business leaders, Santa Claus is relocating his entire operation from the North Pole to the Sunshine State. That's the big reveal in a light-hearted holiday video from Sachs Media Group that focuses on efforts to avert a crisis by luring the season’s favorite Man in Red to Florida.
