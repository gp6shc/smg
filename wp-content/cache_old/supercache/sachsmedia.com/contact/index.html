Internship Opportunities

Sachs Media Group offers competitive and rewarding internships for students interested in pursuing a career in public relations, advertising, design, and/or video production. We have internships available in both Tallahassee and Orlando offices. We seek self-motivated students to become a part of Florida's most aggressive and accomplished communications firm. Our interns work side-by-side with our experienced team of award-winning communicators in a fast-paced and fun environment. From writing news releases and organizing news conferences to scripting television spots and pitching stories to local and national media organizations, our interns get unparalleled real-life experience. Unpaid internships are available for the spring, summer and fall semesters. We prefer interns to work 20 hours per week; however, we sometimes accept interns who wish to work fewer hours.Preference is given to juniors and seniors in a related field of study with strong written and verbal communication skills, though any interested student is encouraged to apply. To apply, please submit your resume and writing sample(s) to Erica Villanueva (internships@sachsmedia.com) for opportunities in Tallahassee or Beth Watson (bwatson@sachsmedia.com) for opportunities in Orlando.

Our Elevator Speech

Sachs Media Group is Florida's dominant public affairs communications company for critical issues and one of America’s leading independent communications companies. In 2011, the firm was named the "PR Agency of the Year" nationally in the prestigious Bulldog Awards. With exceptional experience and results in public affairs, branding, social/digital and crisis communications, the firm combines unparalleled relationships, news judgment, messaging and storytelling ability with cutting-edge strategies to engage audiences with content they seek and share. Sachs Media Group, formerly Ron Sachs Communications, is home to the team best known for smart, strong and strategic counsel across the diverse and ever-changing media landscape.
